Welcome

Welcome to my website. I am a researcher at the intersection of the Social Studies of Science & Technology and Computer Science. I am researching algorithmic, data-driven and so-called 'AI' systems and their relations to society at large. In a time of increased automation and an era of digital transformation I consider it important to critically investigate how these systems shape our lives, but also how they are shaped in turn.
